About Me


I am Robert Makłowicz!

Culinary Enthusiast: Infusing Flavor into Every Dish

Polish journalist, food critic, writer, columnist and traveller. At the turn of 1989 and 1990 he lived in the United Kingdom, where he worked for several months as a labourer in Kingston upon Thames. During his stay in exile, he got to know various cuisines of the world, which, after his return to Poland in 1990, led him to become a food critic. In March 2020, he launched his own YouTube channel, where he publishes videos on culinary and historical topics. In 2022, he became an ambassador of the Wrocław Year of Good Relations, a campaign to draw attention to the growing problem of loneliness.
